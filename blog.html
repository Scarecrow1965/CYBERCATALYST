    BLOG 1

    Ensuring cyber safety is paramount during your job search. Here are this week’s tips:
    • Keep your software updated and secure; outdated versions can be vulnerable to cyber threats.
    • Invest in reliable anti‐malware software to safeguard your device from potential risks.
    • Establish a dedicated email account for job applications to shield your personal inbox from unwanted solicitations.
    • Prioritize research on employers and websites before sharing personal information; ensure their legitimacy.
    • Steer clear of public Wi‐Fi when submitting applications or accessing sensitive accounts. If you must, remember to log out and clear your browser history and cache.

    Fortinet, a global cybersecurity leader, has taken a significant step towards empowering our Military Veteran Community with the formation of the Veterans Program Advisory Council. The initiative focuses on creating more cybersecurity opportunities for the Military Veteran Community.
     
    We’re pleased to announce that Patrick Shaw, Founder of Cyber Catalyst Talent Solutions, has been included as the board member representing Canada, joining a distinguished global panel of members from across the Five Eyes countries: the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, New Zealand, and Australia. This group will provide guidance to Fortinet in meeting the needs of military veterans looking to transition into the field of cybersecurity.
     
    Patrick is excited to be included in Fortinet’s commitment to securing the digital landscape and supporting those who have served our nations.
